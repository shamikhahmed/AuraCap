# AuraCap — Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** June 11, 2026

AuraCap is an offline-first Progressive Web App for organizing Apple ecosystem app setups. **Your app lists, layouts, and preferences stay on your device** unless you explicitly export them.

## What we collect

- **Nothing by default.** No accounts, analytics SDKs, or third-party trackers ship in this app.
- Smart Assistant features (DNA analysis, chat, layout recommendations) run entirely on-device with rules-based logic — no external LLM or API calls.

## Storage

- App state is stored in **IndexedDB** and **localStorage** in your browser.
- Wallpaper and app catalog data is bundled with the app.
- Uninstalling or clearing site data removes your imported apps and custom layouts.

## Network

- GitHub Pages serves static files only — no server-side access to your data.
- AuraCap does not upload your app list anywhere.

## Imports & exports

- App lists you paste or import are processed locally on your device.
- JSON backups are generated locally — you choose where to save or share them.
